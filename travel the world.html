KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Tamil Nadu, which showcases the mesmerizing nature around. Kodaikanal's forests are not a place with scenic views, but are also perfect for long walks, and unwinding. You can spend some relaxing moments in the natural surroundings amongst the beautiful trees around. Therefore, if you want to break away from your daily routine and relax for a while, this is the place to be.


HAMPI

Hampi is an ancient village in the south Indian state of Karnataka. It's dotted with numerous ruined temple complexes from the Vijayanagara Empire. On the south bank of the River Tungabhadra is the 7th-century Hindu Virupaksha Temple, near the revived Hampi Bazaar. A carved stone chariot stands in front of the huge Vittala Temple site. Southeast of Hampi.
